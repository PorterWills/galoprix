The Championship

The world's oldest sport.
Finally crowned.

Racing runs across every continent. Billions move through its betting pools every week. And yet it has no global league, no world champion, no season.

Galoprix changes that. Nation vs nation. Jockeys as stars. A format built for the streaming era, where tribalism drives fandom and every race matters.

Horse racing moves over $110 billion globally across betting, sponsorship and media every year. The sport has the economics of a global industry and the structure of a domestic one. Galoprix closes that gap.

Galoprix is structured as a franchise league. Revenue flows from franchise fees, betting partnerships, broadcast rights and title sponsorship. The model is built. The window is open.

Instant Fandom
